## QA Analysts

### Role Summary
QA Analysts own quality assurance strategy, test planning, and validation of acceptance criteria. They ensure deliverables meet quality standards before release and collaborate closely with developers and product teams.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and execute test plans aligned with acceptance criteria
- Perform functional, integration, and regression testing
- Identify and document defects with clear reproduction steps
- Collaborate with developers to resolve quality issues
- Define Definition of Done criteria from a quality perspective
- Participate in release readiness assessments

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and user satisfaction
- Reduce defects reaching production
- Enable confident, predictable releases

### Interaction Points
- **With Developers**: review acceptance criteria, validate PRs, triage test failures
- **With Product Managers**: clarify requirements and edge cases for test coverage
- **With Project Managers**: provide quality status in weekly syncs and release readiness reports
- **In Execution**: participate in sprint planning, daily standups, and pre-release verification

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and test case documentation
- Defect reports and triage discussions
- Release readiness sign-offs
- Quality metrics and trend analysis
